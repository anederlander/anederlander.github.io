I am a PhD candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Stony Brook University, advised by Dr. Arie Kaufman. My research focuses on augmented reality, medical visualization, and image-guided surgical systems, with applications in ophthalmology and abdominal surgery.

I develop interactive systems that integrate imaging data with real-time visualization to support clinical decision-making and surgical guidance.
